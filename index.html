Welcome to My Writings Archive!

Welcome to my little corner of the internet, where I'll be uploading my writings that done, or are not quite finished but finished enough. I don't like posting straight to social media because it feels in-your-face, so I figured I'd have a place that let people keep up with my work if they wanted to! I also coded it all by hand as an art project of its own, so I hope you have fun navigating it and finding all the little secrets! If you don't want to explore, just use the menu in the top corner to navigate by category. Thanks for reading!
